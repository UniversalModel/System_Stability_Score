MLS ORGANIZATION RIGHTS
What Every Member Can Expect
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

Right to Know Your Role:
Every member knows what is expected. Clear roles, clear goals.

Right to Necessary Resources:
Equipment, facilities, information, and support needed to do your job.

Right to Coaching and Development:
Quality instruction. Regular feedback. Opportunities to improve.

Right to Fair Evaluation:
Performance judged by consistent standards. Merit, not politics.

Right to Recognition:
When you perform well, it gets noticed. Contributions acknowledged.

Right to Be Heard:
Your opinions matter. You can speak up. Leadership listens.

Right to Respect:
Treated with dignity regardless of role or salary. No harassment.

Right to Health and Safety:
Safe facilities. Quality medical care. Injury prevention.

Right to Know Where You Stand:
Honest feedback about your status. What do you need to improve?

Right to Career Development:
Help planning your future. The club helps you grow.

Right to Work-Life Balance:
Soccer demands a lot. But family matters too.

Right to Fair Treatment:
Equal standards for everyone. DPs and homegrowns follow same rules.

Right to Privacy:
Personal matters stay personal. Medical info confidential.

Right to Due Process:
Fair hearing if there is a problem. Both sides heard.

Right to Purpose:
Know how your role contributes to the bigger picture.
